Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is important to accommodate those who do not have the latest and greatest pieces of tech. It also helps us web developers to create solid content for the website. The message/content conveyed is the most important aspect of the website, as that is typically what users are looking for when they visit the site. The Css and javascript are the nice, but ultimately unecessary. The main focus of a website should be to convey the content, and the rest is used to make it look official, and presentable. The reason this is the main focus is to accomadte users whom own older tech, so they still may access the site. The css and javascript it to make the site presentable and nice looking.
